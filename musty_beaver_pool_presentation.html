About us

A New Era of Swimming

The Musty Beaver Swimming Pool is the newest addition to our neighbourhood sports centre. Our state-of-the-art facility has been designed with every member of the community in mind — from toddlers taking their first splash to seniors keeping active.


🏊
25-metre Pool
Olympic-standard lanes, temperature-controlled water at 28°C year-round.
🚿
Modern Facilities
Spacious changing rooms, lockers, showers, and a poolside lounge.
♿
Fully Accessible
Ramps, hoists, and adapted programmes for all abilities and needs.

Why swimming?

The Benefits

01
Full-body workout, zero impact Swimming works every major muscle group while protecting your joints — ideal for all ages and fitness levels.
02
Cardiovascular & respiratory health Regular swimming strengthens your heart, improves lung capacity, and reduces the risk of chronic disease.
03
Mental wellbeing Water has a proven calming effect. Swimming reduces stress, anxiety, and helps you sleep better.
04
Community & social connection Group classes and shared sessions create friendships and a sense of belonging right in your neighbourhood.
